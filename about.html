About Us

Hey there! We're FlaskAndSuch, a small crew that started in 2020 with a simple idea: make water bottles that are as cool as you are. We’re all about crafting sturdy, planet-friendly bottles that keep your coffee hot or your water ice-cold, no matter where your day takes you. From sweaty gym sessions to lazy park picnics, we’ve got your back. Let’s stay hydrated and keep it real!
